Dissertation Research

My three-aim dissertation evaluates how copay caps and scope-of-practice reform affect insulin utilization, treatment quality, and acute clinical outcomes — unified by Grossman's health capital model and powered by IQVIA PharMetrics Plus data.

Aim 1 • Federal Policy

Propensity score matching evaluation of the $35/month federal cap on insulin copays — effects on adherence, formulation switching, and acute events.
